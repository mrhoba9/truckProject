Sweden Trading - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of truck parts do you sell?

We sell a wide range of truck parts including engine components, suspension systems, steering parts, lighting and electrical components, brake parts, and body parts like bumpers and mirrors. We cater to both heavy-duty and light-duty trucks.

Do you offer warranties on the parts?

Yes, most of our parts come with a manufacturer's warranty. Warranty periods and terms vary depending on the part and manufacturer, but we strive to offer only high-quality parts with reliable guarantees.

How can I find the right part for my truck?

You can use our easy search tool on the homepage to look up parts by truck make, model, and year. If you're unsure or need assistance, our expert customer service team is here to help you find the perfect match for your vehicle.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping on most of our products. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on the destination. Contact our support team for more details regarding your location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track the status of your order through our website or directly on the shipping carrier's site.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a refund or exchange. The item must be in its original condition and packaging. Certain restrictions may apply, especially for special-order items.

Can I get a discount on bulk orders?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. If you're purchasing multiple parts or large quantities, please contact our sales team to inquire about bulk discounts and offers.

How do I contact customer support?
